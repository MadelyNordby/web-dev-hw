Baked Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 6 C Quick Oats
  • 2 C Packed Brown Sugar
  • 4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 2 C Milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Grease a 9x13" pan, set aside.
  3. Combine all the dry ingredient and mix well.
  4. Combine wet ingredients to the dry mixture, mix thoroughly.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until oatmeal is set.
  6. ENJOY!
