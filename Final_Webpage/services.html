Count the Views - Not the Miles

Adventures are Healthy!

Going outside and having our own personal 'adventures' is a HUGE asset to our daily lives and wellbeing. There is a document by Harvard Health Publishing that lays out some of the big benefits for being outdoors. Harvard Health points out 5 ways that being more outdoorsy can improve your life. These include

  1. Increase of Vitamin D
  2. Increase of exercise (even without trying!)
  3. Increase of Happiness
  4. Increase in concentration
  5. You may heal faster

If the views and the memories aren't enough, these are some pretty solid reasons to get outdoors more and find that adventure that makes your day even better.
